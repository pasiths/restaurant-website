Lamprais

Price: $18.99

Serves: 2 people

Ingredients:

  • Rice
  • Meat Curry
  • Brinjal Pahi (Eggplant)
  • Frikkadels (Meatballs)
  • Banana Leaf

Description:

Lamprais is a traditional Sri Lankan Dutch Burgher dish, consisting of rice, meat curry, and several side dishes, all wrapped in a banana leaf and baked. This dish is a flavorful and aromatic combination, often served on special occasions.

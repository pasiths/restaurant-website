Indiappa (String Hoppers)

Price: $18.99

Serves: 2-3 people

Ingredients:

  • Rice Flour
  • Water
  • Salt

Description:

Indiappa, also known as String Hoppers, are steamed rice flour noodles, served with curry and sambol. A popular breakfast or dinner option in Sri Lanka, this dish is light, nutritious, and full of flavor.

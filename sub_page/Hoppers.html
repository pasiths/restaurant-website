Hoppers

Price: $10.99

Serves: 1 person

Ingredients:

  • Rice Flour
  • Coconut Milk
  • Yeast
  • Salt

Description:

Hoppers, also known as "appa," are a traditional Sri Lankan dish made from a fermented batter of rice flour and coconut milk. They have crispy edges and a soft, spongy center, and are often served with sambol, a spicy condiment made from ground coconut, and various curries.
